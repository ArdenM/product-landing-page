Features

Download and browse content anywhere, anytime. Just add an item to your library and download it onto your Hub. You can easily remove it from your device, but don't worry - it will remain in your library for download if you ever need it again.
Display two documents side-by-side in a split screen format. Pull up a text and translation side-by-side for reference, or review a referenced text as you read an article.
Our markup feature allows you to highlight and annotate texts as you read. Markup two documents in split-screen and use the Associate feature to easily find connected texts and annotations in multiple documents.
You can set our search feature to look through just the documents on your Hub, your entire library, or all the documents within ClassicsHub. You can even use it to search through your highlights and annotations to pull up all texts related through Associate.
